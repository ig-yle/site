POLYMOSIA COMMUNITY

Esplorare Residences, meaning "Explore Residences," is the centerpiece of the Polymosia community. It offers diverse living experiences, encouraging residents to explore the rich tapestry of communal life within Florence.

Piazza Paradiso Towers, or "Paradise Square Towers," stand tall as a symbol of communal bliss. This community hub within Polymosia is where residents gather to create lasting memories and connections.

Sereno Quartiere Homes, translating to "Serene Neighborhood Homes," is the embodiment of peace within the Polymosia community. It provides a tranquil living space where residents can enjoy a serene atmosphere.
