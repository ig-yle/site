HOTEL AND LEISURE

Lussuoso Resort, meaning "Luxury Resort," epitomizes opulence and leisure in Florence. It offers a haven of luxury, ensuring a delightful stay for guests seeking an indulgent experience.

Dolce Vita Hotels, translating to "Sweet Life Hotels," provide a taste of the sweet life for guests visiting Florence. With a blend of comfort and luxury, it promises an unforgettable stay.

Rilassante Retreats, or "Relaxing Retreats," offer a peaceful escape for those seeking tranquility in Florence. With serene surroundings and impeccable service, it's a retreat for relaxation and rejuvenation.
