OFFICE AND RETAIL

Centrale Affari Plaza, or "Central Business Plaza," is the heartbeat of business and retail in Florence. With its strategic location, it serves as a central hub for commerce, providing a dynamic environment for growth.

Bottega Brio Complex, meaning "Shop Vivacity Complex," is a vibrant retail and office space in the heart of Florence. It's a lively and dynamic complex that invites businesses and shoppers alike.

Innovare Square, translating to "Innovate Square," is the epicenter of innovation in Florence. This office and retail space fosters creativity and forward-thinking, creating a vibrant atmosphere for businesses to thrive.
