POLYMOSIA SUBDIVISIONS

Villetta Vista Estates, or "Cottage View Estates," introduces a unique charm to the Polymosia subdivision. With its picturesque cottages and modern amenities, it offers a blend of classic and contemporary living.

Giardino Giocoso Residences, meaning "Playful Garden Residences," transforms the Polymosia subdivision into a playful haven. It's a place where families can enjoy a vibrant and interactive living experience.

Colline Charme Villas, translating to "Hill Charm Villas," bring a touch of charm to the Polymosia subdivision. Nestled on the hills, these villas offer a unique and enchanting living experience.
